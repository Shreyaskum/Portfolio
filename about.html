About Me

BIO

I am a graduate student at Santa Clara University currently pursuing masters in Computer Science Engineering. My past experience as a Software Engineer at WIPRO Ltd., has given me a solid understanding of architecting web-based and data-driven applications and services. Initially I was a part of Java Backend development team and later I moved to full stack python project. I was also a part of a networking team where I configured client devices using Software Defined Networking. Working with a team of 4 engineers, I was given complete ownership not just for implementing but also testing and deploying the service. My team followed the Agile model of development to deliver under tight deadlines. We also used GIT to deploy our projects in code cloud. In addition to this, I collaborated closely with the product team to decide the initial design requirements for the product and better understand the user experience. I feel that with my current skillset and broad exposure to technical frameworks, I can contribute to solving complex problems at scale.
